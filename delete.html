Data Deletion Request

Your privacy matters to us. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. We will process all deletion requests within 30 days.

How to Request Data Deletion

  1. Send an email to our support team using the button below
  2. Include your registered email address in the request
  3. We will confirm receipt within 2 business days
  4. Your account and all data will be permanently deleted within 30 days

Please use the subject line Data Deletion Request and include the email address associated with your EmergingMarketIQ account.

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon receiving your request we will permanently delete:

  • Your account credentials and profile information
  • Your name and email address
  • Your subscription history and preferences
  • Any usage data associated with your account

Timeline

We will process your deletion request within 30 days of receipt. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been permanently deleted.
