✦ Data Deletion Request

Request Account & Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your EmergingMarketIQ account and all associated personal data at any time. We will process all verified deletion requests within 30 days.

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Your account email address and display name
  • Your account profile and preferences
  • Your subscription history and status
  • Your app usage and activity data
  • Any saved searches or bookmarks
  • All authentication tokens and sessions

Please note: Once your account is deleted this action cannot be undone. You will lose access to any active premium subscription without a refund for the remaining billing period. If you have an active Google Play subscription please cancel it separately through Google Play before requesting deletion.

How to Submit a Deletion Request

1
Send us an email

Use the button below to send a deletion request from the email address associated with your EmergingMarketIQ account.

2
We verify your identity

We will send a confirmation email to verify that the request came from the account owner.

3
We process the deletion

Once verified we will delete all your data within 30 days and send you a confirmation.

Processing Timeline

We aim to process all deletion requests within 30 days of receiving your verified request. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.
