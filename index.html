EmergingMarketIQ Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

EmergingMarketIQ is operated by Mustansir Yaqub based in Melbourne, Australia.

Information We Collect

We collect your email address and name when you create an account. We collect usage data including which countries and features you access within the app.

How We Use Your Information

We use your email address to authenticate your account and send important service updates. We use usage data to improve the app experience. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

AI Generated Content

Premium intelligence features are powered by Anthropic's Claude API. Queries you submit to the AI assistant are processed by Anthropic's systems in accordance with their privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy.

Data Storage

Your account data is stored securely using Supabase infrastructure hosted on AWS. Data is stored in accordance with applicable Australian privacy laws.

In-App Purchases

Premium subscriptions are processed securely through Google Play. We do not store your payment information. Subscription management is handled through your Google Play account.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.
